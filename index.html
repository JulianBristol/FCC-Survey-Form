Welcome to Milkshake's Cat Café

Because cats and coffee makes the world go round

Milkshake's Cat Café strives to engage you with an experience that cannot be recreated any where. Our selection of teas, coffee, and other treats are formulated to evigorate the sense and banish the stresses accumulated from everyday life.

Our affection associates (our cats and kittens) are trained to enforce an environment of peace and leisure. We offer a selection of cat treats with every order so that you can tip your favorite Affection Associate. You can also use any of the provided cat toys free of charge.

All around, an experience here are Milkshake's Cat Café transends the physical realm and heals the spiritual body. Watch your negative emotions melt away with the therapuetic aroma of our fresh tea and coffee blends. Be enveloped in childlike bliss with our furry friends.

We are always looking for suggestions. Please fill out the form below to tell us your thoughts.

As a bonus, you will also recieve a 10% discount coupon on selected items for your next visit.
